hctsa-UMAP projections of UAE/UCR datasets

Below are some umap projections of UEA/UCR time-series classification datasets.

In each dataset, each time series is represented in the high-dimensional feature space of hctsa features. In each plot, we show the data projected into a two-dimensional umap space. Each point in the space is a time series, and we have colored each point according to its assigned label. In many cases, the unsupervised umap projection captures the labeled structure of the data.

Only a selection of the most interesting-looking projections (according to our eyes) are shown here. Oh, did you want to have a play with all the data? It's available on our figshare repository.
